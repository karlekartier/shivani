Professional Journey

My career began with a comprehensive Pharm.D program, where I developed a deep understanding of pharmacology and patient care. During my one-year internship at ESI Hospital, I gained invaluable hands-on experience in clinical pharmacy, ward rounds, and adverse drug reaction (ADR) monitoring.

To further enhance my expertise, I completed an Advanced Diploma in Clinical Research from CLRI, covering medical writing, pharmacovigilance, clinical research methodologies, regulatory affairs, and clinical data management.
